# Mini ERP - Demand to Delivery

A production-grade ERP system for furniture manufacturing companies.

Core Modules:
1. Authentication & RBAC
2. Product Management
3. Inventory Management
4. Sales Orders
5. Purchase Orders
6. Bill Of Materials (BoM)
7. Manufacturing Orders
8. Procurement Automation
9. Stock Ledger
10. Audit Logs
11. Dashboard & Reports

Business Flow:

Customer Order
↓
Sales Order
↓
Inventory Check
↓
Make To Stock or Make To Order
↓
Purchase / Manufacturing
↓
Stock Update
↓
Delivery
↓
Audit Log

System must support:
- Make To Stock (MTS)
- Make To Order (MTO)
- Inventory Reservations
- Procurement Automation
- Manufacturing Workflow
- Role Based Access Control
- Real Time Dashboard

Target Users:
- Admin
- Sales User
- Purchase User
- Manufacturing User
- Inventory Manager
- Business Owner
